Evidence Map by Year, Organ & Role in Review

Landscape overview

Description

Each bubble represents one included study, positioned by publication year (x-axis) and organ (y-axis). Bubble colour encodes the study's role in the review: dark green = Core, medium green = Support citation, pale green = Discussion only. Overlapping bubbles are spread vertically for clarity.

Key Findings

Kidney dominates — 25 of 31 included studies (81%) are kidney-only, spanning the full 2020–2026 window.
Cross-organ papers are emerging — Heart, Uterus, and Kidney & Placenta entries all appear from 2023 onwards.
Core studies cluster in 2022–2025, with the most recent Core addition being the Heart study (Giarraputo 2026).

Study Design Heatmap

Methodological diversity

Description

A heatmap with Organ on the y-axis and Study Design category on the x-axis. Cell colour intensity is proportional to the number of studies in that cell; white cells indicate no studies. Row and column totals are shown as marginal annotations.

Design categories were derived from Document Type, Original Research flag, Retro/Prospective flags, Archival FFPE flag, and Method free-text.

Key Findings

Kidney × Retrospective cohort (FFPE) (n=9) and Kidney × Prospective / mixed cohort (n=6) are the two darkest cells — the core evidentiary mass.
Kidney is the only organ represented across all 6 design categories, confirming its methodological maturity.
Heart and Uterus each occupy only 1–2 design cells, highlighting the design-diversity gap.
